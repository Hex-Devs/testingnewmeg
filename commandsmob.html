Commands

Emotions

"sad" - bot sends random GIF with sad emotion.
Syntax: [prefix]sad

"dance" - bot sends random GIF with dance.
Syntax: [prefix]dance

"laugh" - bot sends random GIF with laugh emotion.
Syntax: [prefix]laugh

"happy" - bot sends random GIF with happy emotion.
Syntax: [prefix]happy

"hug" - bot sends random GIF with characters, that are hugging.
Syntax: [prefix]hug [@mentioned_user]

"kiss" - bot sends random GIF with characters, that are kissing.
Syntax: [prefix]kiss [@mentioned_user]

"punch" - bot sends random GIF with character, that punches another one.
Syntax: [prefix]punch [@mentioned_user]

Entertainment

"meg" - bot will reply to your message.
Syntax: [prefix]meg [your_message]

"dice" - bot starts dice game between you and mentioned user.
Syntax: [prefix]dice [@mentioned_user]

"botrps" - bot plays rock-paper-scissors game with you.
Syntax: [prefix]botrps

"rps" - bot starts rock-paper-scissors game between you and mentioned user (60 seconds cooldown).
Syntax: [prefix]rps [@mentioned_user]

Moderation

"emb" - bot creates embed with your custom data.
Syntax: [prefix]emb [simple/advanced] [color_hex_code] [color (only for simple type)] [your message (only for simple type)]

"warn" - bot gives a warn/checks amount of warns/sets your custom amount of warns for mentioned user.
Syntax: [prefix]warn [give/check/set] [@mentioned_user] [reason/nothing/amount]

"timeout" - bot mutes mentioned user.
Syntax: [prefix]timeout [@mentioned_user] [time in minutes] [reason]

"kick" - bot kicks mentioned user.
Syntax: [prefix]kick [@mentioned_user] [reason]

"ban" - bot bans mentioned user.
Syntax: [prefix]ban [@mentioned_user] [reason]

*Info in square brackets means variables, that may be different in each Discord server
